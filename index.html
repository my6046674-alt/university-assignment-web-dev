About StudySync

Created as a college project — designed to make student life simpler, more organized, and less stressful.

We built StudySync because we know how chaotic college assignments can get. Multiple subjects, overlapping deadlines, group projects — it's easy to lose track.

Our goal is simple: give students a clean, modern tool that actually helps instead of adding more complexity.

Currently in development — more features coming soon!
